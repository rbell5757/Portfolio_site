Hello!

My name is Ryan Bell. I am a User Experience Designer located in Orange County, California.

Energetic, enthusiastic, and driven UX Designer with experience designing for a wide variety of markets and products. From tax compliance to insurance advertising to social media, I have been involved in a large number of projects immersed in the principles of user centered design and research.

Seeking positions as a UX/UI Designer where I can utilize my diverse skill set to shape the way that users interact with a product. My process in approaching design projects is to deconstruct the existing concept, analyze from a range of perspectives, reconstruct the elements in a meaningful way, and apply finishing touches to craft a pleasant user experience.
